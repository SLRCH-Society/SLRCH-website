## About the program

The course runs entirely online each year, from fall/winter through spring, and concludes with a final exam held at the SLRCH annual meeting in June. It's designed to build a solid working knowledge of core circadian biology, including the key anatomical structures and genetic mechanisms that drive circadian rhythms.

## Certification

<div class="card">
  <p>Participants who hold clinical credentials can go on to earn a certificate of expertise in chronotherapy and chronomedicine after passing the final exam at the annual meeting.</p>
