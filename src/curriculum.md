## About the program

The program will be fully online and run from Fall/Winter to Spring every year with an online exam at the end of the course. We will host a graduation reception for participants at the annual SLRCH meeting. We anticipate our first cohort to launch this October - stay tuned for more details!

## Program aims:

- Provide participants with the opportunity to complete a training program in chronotherapy and chronomedicine certified by SLRCH.

- For those with clinical credentials, possibility to obtain a certificate of expertise in chronotherapy and chronomedicine after passing a final exam.

- Facilitate networking between members with a graduation reception at our annual conference.


## By the end of the course, participants will:

1. Have a working knowledge of fundamental circadian biology, including major anatomical structures and genetic factors influencing circadian rhythms

2. Gain insight into translational approaches that bridge basic circadian research with clinical applications, including biomarker development, therapeutic timing, chronopharmacology and light-based interventions

3. Understand how to apply chronotherapy to treat circadian rhythm, psychiatric, and neurological disorders, as well as appreciate the opportunities for circadian medicine in clinical practice broadly
